About Stackly

India's trusted local waste management portal — connecting clients with certified waste management companies across Coimbatore, Tirupur, Erode and beyond.

Our Mission

Stackly was founded with a simple belief — managing waste responsibly should not be complicated. We bridge the gap between waste generators and certified waste management companies, making waste disposal local, compliant, and efficient.

We focus on Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities where reliable waste management services are hard to find. Our hyper-local approach ensures that businesses and households connect with certified nearby waste operators, reducing transport costs and environmental impact.

From IT parks in Peelamedu to manufacturing hubs in SIPCOT, from textile units in Tirupur to hospitals on Avinashi Road — we cover every corner of the local waste management ecosystem.
